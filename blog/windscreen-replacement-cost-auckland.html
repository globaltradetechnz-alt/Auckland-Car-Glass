How Much Does a Car Windscreen Replacement Cost in Auckland?

The cost of replacing a car windscreen in Auckland typically ranges from $250 to $500 for most common vehicles. The final price depends on several factors, which we break down below.

What Affects the Cost?

  • Car make and model — Common Japanese imports (Toyota, Nissan, Honda) are usually cheaper than European models (BMW, Mercedes, Audi) due to parts availability.
  • Glass features — Rain sensors, ADAS cameras, heated windscreens, acoustic interlayers and head-up displays increase the cost.
  • Side vs windscreen — Side windows are generally cheaper ($150–$300), while rear glass with heating elements can be $200–$400.
  • Insurance vs private — If you have comprehensive insurance, you typically only pay your excess ($0–$100). We invoice your insurer directly.
  • Mobile service — Our mobile fitting service is included at no extra cost across Auckland.
