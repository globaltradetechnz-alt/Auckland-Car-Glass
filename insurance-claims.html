Windscreen Insurance Claims in New Zealand

We handle car windscreen insurance claims from start to finish. We deal with your insurer, invoice them directly, and get you back on the road with minimal paperwork.

How Insurance Claims Work for Car Glass

Most comprehensive car insurance policies in New Zealand cover windscreen, side window and rear glass replacement. The process is straightforward, and we make it even easier by handling the claim on your behalf.

If your policy includes glass cover, you typically only pay the excess (often $0–$100 for glass claims). We invoice your insurance company directly for the full cost of the replacement, so you don't have to pay out of pocket and wait for reimbursement.

Not sure if you're covered? Call us on 09 972-1091 with your policy number and insurer name. We can check your cover in minutes and start the claim straight away.

Insurers We Work With

We are approved repairers and work directly with all major New Zealand car insurance providers:

State Insurance

AMI Insurance

AMI's comprehensive policies cover windscreen and glass replacement. Call AMI on 0800 100 200. We handle the rest.

Vero Insurance

Vero offers glass cover as part of their comprehensive motor policies. Contact Vero on 0800 808 175. We are a Vero-approved repairer.

FMG Insurance

FMG covers glass replacement for private and rural vehicles. Call FMG on 0800 366 466. We work with FMG regularly and know their claim process.

Tower Insurance

Tower's comprehensive car insurance includes windscreen and glass cover. Contact Tower on 0800 808 808. We can start the job as soon as your claim is lodged.

What You Need to Make a Claim

  • Your insurance policy number
  • Car registration number
  • A description of the damage (cracked, chipped, shattered, etc.)
  • When and how the damage happened

Don't have all the details? No problem — call us and we'll help you gather what's needed.
